Family Time
Family time is intended for Adults to play with children in the family. During this time one Adult must be a paid-up Member.
Juniors
Juniors may play in non-Junior times with the understanding that their court must be relinquished if Adults are waiting. Likewise, Adults must relinquish any court to Juniors, other than court 5, in Juniors’ times.
Non-Family Time
Adults playing with Juniors in non-Family times must relinquish their court to Adults who are waiting.

Events posted on the Calendar have priority.
